# Settings Evaluation Session

**Started**: 2025-01-10
**Status**: IN_PROGRESS
**Task**: Comprehensive evaluation of all settings in StickyNotes - where they go, what they tie into, correctness, and improvement opportunities

---

## Approach

Deploying 10 autonomous Sonnet agents to independently explore and evaluate settings across the codebase. Each agent will:

1. Discover settings on their own
2. Track where settings flow through the codebase
3. Identify issues and improvement opportunities
4. Save findings to this document regularly

Agents are NOT pre-assigned areas - they explore autonomously and may overlap, providing diverse perspectives.
